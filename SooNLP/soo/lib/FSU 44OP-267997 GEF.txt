GAS ROOM EF CONTROL

TWO SPEED FAN SHALL RUN CONTINUOUSLY AT LOW CFM SETPOINT. 

LOW CFM SETPOINT = 100 CFM.


HYDROGEN DETECTION ALARM ACTIVATION

TWO POSITION FAN SPEED CONTROL: UPON HYDROGEN DETECTION ALARM ACTIVATION, EXHAUST FAN SHALL MODULATE TO HIGH CFM SETPOINT. 

HIGH CFM SETPOINT = 500 CFM. 

BAS SHALL MONITOR HYDROGEN DETECTION SYSTEM FOR FAULT AND ALARM CONFITIONS.
