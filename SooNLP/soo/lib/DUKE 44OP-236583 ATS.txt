AUTOMATIC TRANSFER SWITCH MONITORING - CONTROL SEQUENCE -(PER SPEC 230993.3.10)

A.	Building automation system shall monitor and alarm the position of the following automatic transfer switches. Visual indication of the transfer switch positions shall be viewable from the BAS.
1.	ATS-LR1
2.	ATS-LR2
3.	ATS-OP1
4.	ATS-E
B.	BAS shall also monitor a failed-to-sync alarm at each of the automatic transfer switches.
